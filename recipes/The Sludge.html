The Sludge.

The Sludge. Rend thyself of thy turmoil, banish this evil that doth come upon thee. Don't Mess This Up.

Requirments

  • Sacred Swamp Mud
  • 2 Crow Feathers
  • Purified Water
  • Betty Crocker Fudge Brownie Mix - 16.3oz

The Process

  1. Over an occultic flame, mix together the feathers and water
  2. Once the water has become pitch black, mix in the mud. It will be finished once the mixture starts turning thick
  3. Let cool
  4. Follow recipe on the Betty Crocker Fudge Brownie Mix - 16.3oz
  5. Have brownies
